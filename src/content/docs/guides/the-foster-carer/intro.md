Every fostering household is supported by a qualified Supervising Social Worker.

Your SSW will:

- Visit you regularly (minimum monthly unless agreed otherwise)
- Maintain weekly contact (usually by phone)
- Support you in emergencies
- Attend key meetings where required
- Complete your Annual Foster Carer Review
- Monitor records and standards

Your SSW is your first point of contact for:

- Safeguarding concerns
- Placement issues
- Education concerns
- Health matters
- Behaviour challenges
- Complaints

You are never expected to manage alone.
