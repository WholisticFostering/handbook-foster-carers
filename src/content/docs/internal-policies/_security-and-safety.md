## ğŸ”‘ Office Security

**Access Control**

-   Only the [Operations Manager](staff/operations_manager) and
    [Registered Manager](staff/registered_manager) have regular key/code
    access
-   Access details are securely managed --- sharing is not permitted
-   A log of key/code holders is maintained and reviewed

**Visitor Management**

-   All visitors must be pre-approved by the Ops or Registered Manager
-   Visitors sign in/out and must be accompanied at all times
-   Unscheduled visits are not permitted

**Security Systems**

-   The office is protected by locks and may include alarms or CCTV
-   All security equipment is tested regularly
-   Any security breaches must be reported immediately to the landlord
    and recorded via [incident reporting
    procedures](procedures/incident_reporting)

## ğŸ”¥ Health & Safety

**Fire Safety**

-   Staff are familiar with fire exits, alarms, and extinguishers
-   Fire drills are held in coordination with the landlord
-   Exits must remain clearly marked and unobstructed

**First Aid**

-   A stocked first aid kit is maintained
-   At least one staff member is trained in first aid and CPR
-   Emergency numbers are displayed prominently

**Workplace Safety**

-   Regular [risk assessments](staff/risk_assessments) are conducted
-   Office equipment and furniture are checked for safety
-   Good housekeeping (e.g. tidy walkways, clear cables) is encouraged

**Emergency Response**

-   Emergency procedures cover fire, illness, break-ins, and more
-   All staff are trained on how to respond to urgent situations

**Confidentiality**

-   Access to sensitive information is restricted
-   Physical files are shredded when no longer needed
