Wholistic Fostering is committed to creating a safe, inclusive, and
empowering environment for all children, carers, staff and stakeholders.
This policy outlines our approach to promoting equality, celebrating
diversity, and ensuring inclusive practices across all aspects of our
work.

## ğŸ¯ Our Vision and Mission

Wholistic Fostering was founded with a mission to improve outcomes for
children and young people of African and Caribbean heritage, who have
historically experienced poorer outcomes in the care system. We strive
to offer identity-affirming, culturally competent care that recognises
and responds to the unique needs of each child.

We are equally committed to ensuring that every child, regardless of
background, is valued, respected and given every opportunity to thrive.

## ğŸ§­ Principles

All individuals will be treated with dignity and respect.

We proactively challenge discrimination, harassment, and inequality.

Our policies and practices are shaped by the lived experiences of those
we serve.

We promote equal access and participation for everyone.

We celebrate difference and build on diversity as a strength.

## ğŸ’¡ Positive Action

As part of our mission, we may take lawful positive action under Section
158 of the Equality Act 2010 to address underrepresentation or
disadvantage.

This includes targeted recruitment, outreach, and tailored training to
ensure carers are equipped to meet the cultural, emotional and identity
needs of African and Caribbean children in care.

This is done in a way that complements our broader commitment to
diversity and fairness, not at its expense.
