Wholistic Fostering promotes respectful, consistent, and child-centred
behaviour management in all foster placements. We support children and
young people to understand their behaviour, develop self-regulation
skills, and build positive relationships.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ðŸŽ¯ Aims

-   Ensure consistent, non-punitive approaches across all foster homes
-   Promote positive behaviour through trust, recognition, and support
-   Respond constructively to challenging behaviour
-   Help children feel safe, valued, and understood

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ðŸŒ± Core Principles

-   **Respect & Dignity** -- treat every child as an individual with
    worth and voice
-   **Positive Reinforcement** -- reward desired behaviours and growth
-   **Consistency** -- apply approaches fairly and reliably across
    settings
-   **Individualised Responses** -- tailor support to each child's
    background and needs
-   **Constructive, Not Punitive** -- avoid punishment and focus on
    resolution and healing

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ðŸ§° Proactive Strategies

-   **Clear Expectations** -- agreed upfront and regularly reinforced
-   **Positive Relationships** -- build trust and safety through warm,
    honest engagement
-   **Role Modelling** -- carers model calm, respectful behaviour
-   **Environment** -- create nurturing, low-stress home settings
-   **Open Communication** -- regular check-ins to talk about feelings
    and behaviour

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ðŸ”„ Interventions for Challenging Behaviour

-   **Early Intervention** -- act on early signs to prevent escalation
-   **Conflict Resolution** -- use age-appropriate tools to resolve
    disagreements
-   **Time-Out** -- provide calming space when needed, never as
    punishment
-   **Restorative Practice** -- help children take responsibility and
    repair relationships
-   **Support Plans** -- create individual [Behaviour Support
    Plans](resources/forms/behaviour_support_plan) for ongoing needs
