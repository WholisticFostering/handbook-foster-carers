### Children and Young People:

Children must not consume alcohol unless:

- Written permission is provided by their social worker.
- It is for a special occasion (e.g., Christmas lunch).

If a child appears to be under the influence:

- Offer water or squash.
- Monitor their condition closely.
- Contact a GP or go to A&E if needed.
- Never ignore the situation.

### Foster Carers:

## ğŸš¨ In an Emergency

If a child is misusing drugs, alcohol, or solvents:

- Make the situation safe.  
- Call 999 if there's immediate danger or a serious offence may occur.  
- Give first aid if needed.  
- Let fresh air circulate (especially with solvents).  
- Remove harmful items if safe to do so.  
- **Then contact your Supervising Social Worker or the Team Manager.**  

Never take further action without professional guidance.
