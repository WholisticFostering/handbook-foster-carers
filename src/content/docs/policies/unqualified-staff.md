---
title: Unqualified Staff Policy
---


Where unqualified staff and volunteers carry out social work functions
they do so under the direct supervision of experienced social workers,
who are accountable for their work.

This includes Student Social Workers.

The definition of social work functions includes but is not limited to:

      - Home Visits
      - Assessments
      - Supervision
      - Facilitating Contact

Roles which must only be performed by a Qualified Social Worker include
but are not limited to:

      - Supervising Social Workers
      - Fostering Panel Chairs
      - Registered Managers
