**Important reminders:**

-   Always use the right car seat, even for short journeys.
-   The harness should fit snugly --- you should only be able to slide
    two fingers between the chest and the strap.
-   Only use **EU-approved** seats (look for an 'E' in a circle).
-   Deactivate front airbags before fitting a **rear-facing baby seat**
    in the front.
