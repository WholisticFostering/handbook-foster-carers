## ğŸ“š Training for Newly Approved Carers

All newly approved foster carers must:

-   Complete the **TSD Standards Workbook** within 12 months of approval
    (or 18 months for connected persons).
-   Attend induction training, including **Safeguarding**, **Safer
    Caring**, **First Aid**, and **Recording**.
-   Have a personal development plan (PDP) that is regularly reviewed in
    supervision.

-   Maintain a training log and review training needs in their annual
    foster carer review.
