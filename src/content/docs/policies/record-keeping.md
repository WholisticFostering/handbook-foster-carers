title: Record Keeping Policy
---


This policy clarifies the purpose, format and content of information to
be kept on our service's files, on\...

-   the child's files
-   case files relating to foster carers

Child File\'s

We keep the following information on Children / Young People stored in
our Charms system including but not necessarily limited to\...

-   Personal Details (Name, Ethnicity, Dob, etc.)
-   Health Information
-   Placement History Information
-   Legal Status re: Removal
-   Relevant Information relating to Sibling\'s
-   Care Plan Information
-   Placement Agreement Information ie Views, Wishes & Feelings of
    Children/Young People
-   Personal Education Plan Information
-   Child Observation Information
-   Communication Records
-   Information relating to Respite / Short Breaks
-   Information relating to Schedule 6/7

Case files relating to carers are covered in this policy
<http://wiki.wholisticfostering.co.uk/doku.php/policies/record_keeping_-_for_foster_carers>
