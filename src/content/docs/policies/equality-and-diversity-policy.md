title: Equal Opportunities
---


## - Scope

This Policy covers both current and future employees, as well as
businesses and visitors to the West Midlands, who use our services. In
particular, the Policy covers persons who share one or more of the nine
protected characteristics identified in the Equality Act 2010 i.e. age,
disability, gender reassignment, marriage and civil partnership,
pregnancy and maternity, race, religion or belief, gender and sexual
orientation.

## - General Statement

      - Wholistic Fostering are committed to equality of opportunity and believes that the diversity of a community is a major strength which contributes to the success of the organisation. \\ <font 9pt/arial;;#FFFFFF;;#ffffff>.</font> 
      - Wholistic Fostering commits to ensure that its carers, service users and the wider team have equal access to services and are not discriminated against in any aspect of its service delivery. \\ <font 9pt/arial;;#FFFFFF;;#ffffff>.</font> 
      - It will also ensure fair and equal access in employment ranging from recruitment and selection to learning and development opportunities. \\ <font 9pt/arial;;#FFFFFF;;#ffffff>.</font> 
      - Wholistic Fostering is committed to ensuring that its services are available and accessible to current and potential service users. \\ <font 9pt/arial;;#FFFFFF;;#ffffff>.</font> 
      - We will ensure that the range and quality of services provided are appropriate and sensitive to the needs of our staff, carers and the looked after children and young people we serve; \\ <font 9pt/arial;;#FFFFFF;;#ffffff>.</font> 
      - No-one within Wholistic Fostering is subjected to unjustifiable discrimination. \\ <font 9pt/arial;;#FFFFFF;;#ffffff>.</font> 
      - We will promote equality, diversity and social inclusion amongst our partners in the services they provide. \\ <font 9pt/arial;;#FFFFFF;;#ffffff>.</font> 
      - This will be achieved by Wholistic Fostering: \\ <font 9pt/arial;;#FFFFFF;;#ffffff>.</font> 
      - Designing and delivering appropriate, accessible and effective services and facilities to all members of the organisation, while ensuring that equality issues are considered at the outset of all policy development and planning.
      - Providing clear and accessible information about our services, in a variety of appropriate formats
      - Using effective systems for reviewing and monitoring our service delivery so that quality and equality are continuously evaluated and improved.
      - Ensuring that all our employees understand what equality in service provision means by providing specific training;
      - Ensuring that complaints about services will be dealt with promptly
      - We are opposed to all forms of unlawful and unfair discrimination. All job applicants, employees and others who work for us will be treated fairly and will not be discriminated against on any of the above grounds.
      - Decisions about recruitment and selection, promotion, training or any other benefit will be made objectively and without unlawful discrimination.
      - We recognise that the provision of equal opportunities in the workplace is not only good management practice; it also makes sound business sense.
      - Our equal opportunities policy will help all those who work for us to develop their full potential and the talents and resources of the workforce will be utilised fully to maximise the efficiency of the organisation.
      - Staff will not be expected to tolerate discrimination from service users, other employees, providers or any third party.
      - Wholistic Fostering will inform all job applicants, employees and service users of this policy and ensure everyone understands their collective and individual responsibilities.
      - In order to further develop its Equal Opportunities Policy, Wholistic Fostering will monitor and review annually its progress both in employment and service delivery.
