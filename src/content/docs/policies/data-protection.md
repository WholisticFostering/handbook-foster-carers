Wholistic Fostering is committed to complying with the UK GDPR and
relevant data protection laws. We ensure all personal data is handled
lawfully, stored securely, and retained only as long as necessary.

This policy includes:

-   Data protection principles (GDPR compliance)
-   Record retention guidance
-   Breach reporting and incident processes

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ğŸ” GDPR Compliance

**Lawful Basis for Processing**

-   All personal data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently.
-   We document the lawful basis for each processing activity (e.g.
    legal obligation, consent).

**Data Minimisation**

-   We only collect data necessary for our functions.
-   Data is kept relevant, adequate, and limited.

**Accuracy**

-   We take steps to keep personal data accurate and up to date.
-   Incorrect data is corrected or deleted promptly.

**Storage Limitation**

-   Data is only kept for as long as necessary.
-   Our retention schedule defines timeframes for each data type.

**Integrity & Confidentiality**

-   Data is protected from loss, misuse, or unauthorised access.
-   Access is restricted to authorised personnel only.

**Accountability**

-   We maintain records of processing.
-   A designated data protection lead oversees compliance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ğŸ“ Record Retention

Records are retained only as long as necessary and disposed of securely
(e.g. shredding, digital wipe). See below for detailed timeframes.

**Storage & Security**

-   Physical and electronic records are stored securely.
-   Access to records is limited to authorised users as defined in our
    [Staff Code of Conduct](staff/code_of_conduct).

**Disposal**

-   Disposal of records is documented and follows secure procedures.
-   Outdated files are destroyed promptly and in line with regulations.

## ğŸ—‚ Record Retention Schedule

#### 1. Foster Carer Records

-   Application Forms -- 10 years after deregistration
-   Assessment Reports -- 10 years after deregistration
-   Reviews & Supervision -- 10 years
-   Training Records -- 10 years

#### 2. Child & Young Person Records

-   Placement/Care/Health/Education/Contact -- 75 years from date of
    birth

#### 3. Personnel Records

-   Staff files, references -- 6 years after leaving
-   DBS Checks -- record outcome for 6 years
-   Training -- 6 years

#### 4. Admin Records

-   Policies -- current versions indefinitely; old versions 6 years
-   Health & Safety -- 6 years
-   Financial -- 6 years from end of tax year

#### 5. Incident & Complaints

-   Serious Incidents -- 25 years
-   Complaints -- 6 years

#### 6. Training Records

-   Individual Logs -- 10 years
-   Materials -- current indefinitely; old versions 6 years

#### 7. Meeting Minutes

-   Foster Panel -- 10 years
-   Staff Meetings -- 6 years

#### 8. Data Protection

-   Consent Forms -- while in use + 6 years
-   Breach Records -- 6 years

#### 9. General Records

-   Correspondence -- 3 years (longer if linked to a case)
-   Contracts -- 6 years after end date
