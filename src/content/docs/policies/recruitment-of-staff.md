Wholistic Fostering is committed to safeguarding children through the
recruitment of skilled, motivated and suitable staff. We value diversity
and promote equality throughout all our recruitment activity.

## üîç Purpose

This policy ensures that all recruitment:

-   Attracts high-quality applicants
-   Deters unsuitable individuals
-   Identifies the right people to work with children

## üì¢ Stage 1: Advertising

All job adverts include our safeguarding statement:

> \"Wholistic Fostering is committed to safeguarding and promoting the
> welfare of children and expects all applicants to share this
> commitment. All roles are subject to child protection screening and
> Enhanced DBS checks.\"

## üìÑ Stage 2: Application Pack

Applicants receive:

-   [Online Application
    Form](/http///eforms.wholisticfostering.co.uk/employee-forms/wfem01-employee-application-form/)
-   DBS instructions
-   Statement of Purpose
-   Job Description
-   Safer Recruitment Policy

All applications must be completed fully, including the self-disclosure
section.

## üßë‚Äç‚öñÔ∏è Stage 3: Shortlisting

Applications are reviewed systematically. Shortlisted candidates may be
called for a short phone interview before in-person interviews.
References are ideally obtained before the interview.

## ü§ù Stage 4: Interview

At interview, applicants must bring:

-   Photo ID (passport, driving licence)
-   Original certificates of qualifications
-   Right to work evidence
-   Two address documents
-   Two references

Interviews are competency-based. At least one panel member will be
trained in Safer Recruitment. Questions will assess:

-   Safeguarding knowledge
-   Gaps in employment
-   Any DBS concerns
-   Attitudes to working with children

## üì¨ Stage 5: References

References must:

-   Come directly from referees
-   Be verified for authenticity
-   Include checks with previous employers
-   Be followed up if concerns arise

We do not accept open references or testimonials provided by candidates.

## ‚úÖ Stage 6: Final Checks

Before a candidate can start, we verify:

-   Two satisfactory references
-   Identity and qualifications
-   Enhanced DBS clearance
-   Right to work
-   Registration (e.g. Social Work England if relevant)

All documents are logged on the recruitment checklist and stored
securely.

## ‚öñÔ∏è Rehabilitation of Offenders

Posts at Wholistic Fostering are exempt from the Rehabilitation of
Offenders Act 1974. This means:

-   Applicants must declare all convictions unless 'filtered'
-   Failure to disclose relevant convictions may result in dismissal
-   Guidance is available from the Disclosure & Barring Service

We handle disclosures sensitively and confidentially.

## üîê Data Protection

-   Candidates may request to see their references.
-   All recruitment records are handled confidentially.
-   We follow DBS and data protection standards in all vetting.
