Foster carers must:

-   Register children with a GP, dentist, and optician
-   Attend and support regular health checks
-   Promote a healthy lifestyle, emotional wellbeing, and self-care
-   Keep medication stored safely
-   Administer medication appropriately and record all medication given
-   Report any serious illness, injury, or hospitalisation
-   Complete health-related training and refreshers

-   Support carers with relevant training

## ğŸ§¾ Health Plans & Checks

-   Health assessments: within 4 weeks of placement, then every 6 months
    (under 5s) or annually (5+)
-   Dental checks: at least annually
-   SDQs (Strengths & Difficulties Questionnaires): annually for
    children aged 4+
-   Red Book: carers should bring this to all health appointments
-   Health Plans: must be shared with carers and updated as needed

## ğŸ’Š Medication

-   All medication must be stored safely and securely

    given
-   Carers may only administer first aid and non-prescription medication
    where permission has been given (delegated authority)
