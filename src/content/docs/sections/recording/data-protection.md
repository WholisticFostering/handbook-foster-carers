title: Data Protection & Confidentiality
---

Children in care have the right to privacy and confidentiality.

As a foster carer, you are entrusted with sensitive personal information.

You must handle all information:

- Lawfully
- Securely
- Professionally
- Respectfully

Failure to protect information can place children at risk and may result in serious consequences.
