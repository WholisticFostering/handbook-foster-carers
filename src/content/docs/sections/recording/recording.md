Records must be:

- Factual.
- Clear.
- Objective.
- Professional.
- Written promptly (ideally same day).
- Signed and dated.

Use specific descriptions rather than general statements.

Instead of:

> "Child had a bad day."

Write:

> "Child returned from school at 3:30pm appearing withdrawn, did not engage in conversation, and declined dinner. Stated, 'I hate it there.'"
