Children in care often experience complex feelings about relationships.

They may feel loyalty to birth family while also forming attachments within your home.

These mixed emotions can lead to:

- Internal conflict
- Challenging behaviour
- Withdrawal
- Divided loyalties
- Guilt or confusion

Your role is to provide stability without increasing pressure or conflict.
