Young people may:

- Share images impulsively.
- Feel pressured by peers.
- Not understand long-term consequences.

If you discover inappropriate image sharing:

1. Stay calm.
2. Do not shame the child.
3. Do not forward or store images.
4. Inform your Supervising Social Worker immediately.

This may require safeguarding intervention.
