Developing life skills is a key part of preparing children and young people for adulthood.

Life skills should be:

- Age-appropriate
- Gradually introduced
- Consistently supported
- Reflected in care planning

Progress may look different for each child depending on age, trauma history, additional needs, and emotional development.
