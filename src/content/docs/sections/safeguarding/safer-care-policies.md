Safer Care is about creating a home environment that protects:

- The child
- You as foster carers
- Other household members

It supports safe, nurturing relationships while reducing risk and misunderstanding.

Safer Care arrangements should be clear, proportionate, and based on individual risk assessments.

### ğŸš— Transporting Children

When transporting a child:

- Avoid transporting children alone without agreement (where possible).
